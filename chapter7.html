Chapter 7: Multimedia

Multimedia refers to the integration of various forms of content, including text, audio, video, images, and animations. These elements are often used together to create engaging and interactive experiences on websites and applications.

Types of Multimedia:

  • Text: The written content on websites and applications.
  • Audio: Sound used in websites, including music, speech, and sound effects.
  • Video: Moving images, often used for tutorials, entertainment, and advertisements.
  • Images: Static visuals that complement content and provide context.
  • Animations: Moving visuals created to bring elements to life, often used for UI interactions.

Uses of Multimedia:

  • Websites and online platforms
  • Interactive presentations
  • Advertising and marketing
  • Entertainment and education
